Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Neou.io. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our game Neou.io.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Game Data: Your gameplay statistics, scores, achievements, and preferences.
  • Communications: If you contact us, we collect your messages and contact information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Gameplay Information: Game progress, session duration, and in-game actions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser cookies, and error logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the game
  • Process your game progress and save your data
  • Enable multiplayer features and matchmaking
  • Display leaderboards and rankings
  • Send important game updates and announcements
  • Prevent cheating and ensure fair gameplay
  • Analyze game performance and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase

We use Google Firebase for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Storing game data and player statistics
  • Real-time database for multiplayer features

Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Photon PUN 2.0

We use Photon for real-time multiplayer functionality. Their privacy policy is available at photonengine.com/privacy

4.3 Google AdSense

We display ads through Google AdSense. Google may collect information about your interactions with ads. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Neou.io is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the game. We may retain certain information for longer periods as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
