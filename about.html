NEWGAMEHD - Racing Game

NEWGAMEHD offers intense 2D multiplayer racing with immersive 3D perspective. Race against other players, dodge obstacles, and survive for 5 minutes to claim victory on the leaderboards.

Game Features:

  • Real-time Multiplayer Racing: Compete against players worldwide
  • 3D Perspective: Unique visual experience with depth and dimension
  • Survival Gameplay: Dodge cubes and survive for 5 minutes to win
  • Checkpoint System: Automatic save points every minute
  • Distance Leaderboards: Compete for the farthest travel distance

Technical Information

Our games are built using cutting-edge web technologies to ensure the best possible gaming experience:

  • Platform: Unity WebGL (runs directly in browser)

  • Requirements: Modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with WebGL support
  • Storage: No downloads required - play instantly
